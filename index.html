Welcome!

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Roswell Park Comprehensive Cancer Center in Buffalo, NY. My research focuses on understanding how tumor metabolism reshapes the metastatic niche, with a particular emphasis on castration-resistant prostate cancer (CRPC).

I combine approaches from immunometabolism, epigenetics, CRISPR screening, and metabolomics to identify actionable vulnerabilities in aggressive cancers. I'm also interested in how mitochondrial dysfunction and cellular senescence contribute to age-related diseases.

I earned my Ph.D. in Cell Biology & Physiology from the University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences (UAMS) in 2025, where I worked in Dr. Maria Almeida's lab studying the effects of mitochondrial reactive oxygen species and chemotherapy drugs on bone mesenchymal cells.

Feel free to reach out — I'm always open to collaborations and scientific discussions. You can contact me at MdMohsin.Ali@RoswellPark.org.
