Outside the Lab

Science is a big part of my life, but not all of it. Here are some things I enjoy when I'm not in the lab.

🌍 Travel & Exploration

I enjoy exploring new places, learning about different cultures, and experiencing nature. Moving from my home country to the U.S. has been an adventure in itself.

📚 Reading

I like reading about science, history, and personal development. Always open to book recommendations!

🍳 Cooking

Trying out new recipes — especially dishes from home — is one of my favorite ways to unwind after a long day in the lab.

🏃 Fitness

Staying active helps me stay sharp. I try to balance the sedentary lab life with regular exercise.
